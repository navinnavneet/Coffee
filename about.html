About Us

We are a community of passionate coffee hobbyists who research, test and educate around issues relevant to the home barista market. As a community we strive to simplify the mis-information in the home brewing market so that you can make better informed decisions about buying and brewing amazing quality coffee (without having to train as a professional barista).
